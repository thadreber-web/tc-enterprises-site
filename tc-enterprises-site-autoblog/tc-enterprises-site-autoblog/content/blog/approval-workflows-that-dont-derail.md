Three patterns that work:

1. **Single-link proof viewer** so approvers see the latest file without juggling attachments.
2. **Time-boxed approvals** with a clear “approve” or “needs tweaks” deadline.
3. **One-click choices** that capture revision notes in the same place the work happens.

Each keeps production moving without searching through inbox labyrinths. Pair them with automated nudges and your team stays aligned even when the queue is full.
