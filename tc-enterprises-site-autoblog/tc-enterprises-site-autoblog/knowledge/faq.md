## Vector Conversion for Engraving (Primary Service)

**What exactly do you do?**
I convert artwork into production-ready vector files specifically for engraving equipment. You get both single-line EPS files for laser engraving and PLT files with fill patterns for rotary engraving—both formats included with every order.

**What do I receive with each order?**
You get two files: a clean, single-line EPS file optimized for laser engraving, and a PLT file with 7 fill patterns for rotary engraving. Both work seamlessly with Gravostyle software and Gravograph equipment.

**What file formats do you accept?**
I can work with anything you have—PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, EPS, SVG, or even rough sketches. Higher resolution helps, but I can rebuild clean vectors from almost any source.

**What equipment do your files work with?**
The files are optimized for Gravograph engraving systems (M20, LS900, IS400, etc.) running Gravostyle software. The EPS format works with any laser engraver, and the PLT format is standard for rotary systems.

**What's the difference between EPS and PLT?**
EPS files contain single-line outlines perfect for laser engraving. PLT files include fill patterns needed for rotary engraving to properly shade logos. You get both formats included at no extra charge.

**How much does it cost?**
Standard pricing is $40 for simple logos, $45 for typical designs, and $55+ for complex artwork. Rush service is available: 12-hour rush is $60-75, same-day emergency is $90-100. Volume discounts start at 6 files (10% off).

**Do you offer a free trial?**
Yes! First-time customers can request ONE free trial file to test our quality before committing to paid orders. No credit card required.

**How quickly do you turn files around?**
Standard turnaround is 24-48 hours. Rush options include 12-hour service and same-day emergency turnaround for urgent jobs.

**Do you offer volume discounts?**
Absolutely. 6-10 files get 10% off, 11-25 files get 10% off, 26-50 files get 15% off, and 50+ files or monthly retainers get 20% off.

**What if the file doesn't work with my equipment?**
Every file is guaranteed production-ready. If there's an issue I caused, I'll fix it free within 3 business days. I've delivered 26,000+ files over 13 years with a proven track record.

**Can I get revisions?**
Yes—unlimited revisions are included until you're completely satisfied. Changes after your approval are quoted as new work.

**How will I receive the finished files?**
I'll email you both the EPS and PLT files. If you prefer a different delivery method (shared folder, FTP, etc.), just let me know.

**What makes you different from other vector services?**
I specialize exclusively in engraving file preparation—not general graphic design. With 13 years and 26,000+ proven files, I understand engraving equipment, stroke optimization, and production requirements. Every file is guaranteed to work.
