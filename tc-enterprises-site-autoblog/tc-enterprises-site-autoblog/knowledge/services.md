# Services Overview

Use this as a grounding reference when explaining what T & C Enterprises offers. Keep the tone warm, first-person (“I”) and concise while sticking to these facts.

## Artwork & Logo Digitizing
- I rebuild rough inputs into engraver-ready, stroke-only EPS files with a matching preview PNG.
- Editable AI files stay on hand so revisions are quick.
- Diamond-drag formats (PLT/HPGL) and machine-specific templates are available when you tell me your setup.

## Legacy File Rescue
- I normalize older EPS/AI files to remove fills, stray nodes, and double-burn risks.
- Bundled pricing is available if you’re cleaning a back catalog.

## Hybrid Logo Catalog
- Secure, invite-only library with tagging, search, and shareable links.
- Role-based access ensures sales, production, and partners only see what they need.
- Optional human review lets us double-check art before it goes live.

## Automation & Chatbots
- I build chat and workflow automations that stay grounded in documented FAQs, pricing, and policies.
- Integrations can trigger order intake, proof approvals, and status updates inside your production stack.
- Escalations hand off to Discord or email when a human needs to step in.

## Software Projects & Integrations
- Discovery and planning sprints map requirements before any build.
- I ship focused modules—portals, approval flows, or industry-specific tooling like RealtyGenius, CraftCart, or RV Ops.
- Ongoing support retainers cover monitoring, small updates, and roadmap adjustments.
