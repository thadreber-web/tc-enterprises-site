T&C Enterprises Brand Quick Sheet
=================================

Logo Variants:
- Primary: Hexagon + Company Name + Tagline
- Secondary: Hexagon only (icon)
- Monochrome: Black/white version for engraving, watermark, etc.

Color Palette (Suggested):
- Charcoal Black: #1C1C1C (Primary)
- White: #FFFFFF (Contrast)
- Optional Accents (you may adjust later):
  - Laser Red: #E63946
  - Electric Blue: #457B9D
  - Vibrant Orange: #F4A261

Typography:
- Headings: Montserrat Bold (Modern, geometric sans-serif)
- Body: Open Sans Regular (Clean, legible)

Usage Guidelines:
- Keep clear space around the hexagon equal to the height of the 'T' in the wordmark.
- Minimum size: 32px height for digital, 0.5in for print.
- Tagline can be dropped in small applications.

Applications:
- Website header: Use primary logo.
- Social icons & favicon: Use secondary hex-only logo.
- Engraving/watermark: Use monochrome version.
